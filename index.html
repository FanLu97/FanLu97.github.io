Biography

Fan Lu is currently a fourth-year Ph.D. student in ISPC Group of Tongji University and supervised by Prof. Guang Chen. Before that, I received my bachelor’s degree of Automotive Engineering in Tongji University in 2020. My research interests mainly focus on 3D Computer Vision, Point Cloud Registration, and Neural Rendering.
